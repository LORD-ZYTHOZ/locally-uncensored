## Posting Guidelines

- **Read the rules first.** Most servers have #rules or pinned messages with self-promo policies.
- **Post in the right channel.** Use #showcase, #projects, or #share-your-work — never #general unless invited.
- **Be genuine.** Share what you built and why, not a sales pitch. Engage with questions.
- **Include a screenshot or short video.** Visual posts get far more engagement.
- **Stick around.** Answer questions, take feedback, be part of the community.
- **Don't spam.** One post per server. Follow up only if there's genuine discussion.
